Our Vision

The vision of Swami Vivekanand Students’ Welfare Charitable Trust is rooted deeply in the values of equality, education, and empowerment inspired by the teachings of Swami Vivekanand. The founder envisioned a society where no girl student is denied the right to education due to financial barriers or social discrimination.

Through moral, intellectual, and social upliftment, the trust aspires to nurture confident, responsible, and self-reliant women who contribute positively to the growth of their communities and nation.

The Trust further believes that education must be holistic—incorporating ethical values and societal duties alongside academic learning, to prepare students as exemplary citizens.

  • Ensure financial and moral support for underprivileged girls seeking quality education.
  • Foster self-reliance and confidence through scholarships combined with personality development programs.
  • Promote education free from discrimination of caste, creed, or religion.
  • Integrate ethics, duties, and social responsibility in educational initiatives.
  • Encourage holistic development so beneficiaries become proud contributors to society.
  • Maintain transparency, accountability, and need-based support as guiding principles.
